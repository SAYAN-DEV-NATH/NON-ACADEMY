About Bangladesh

Bangladesh 2.0

"Bangladesh 2.0" refers to a period of significant political and social change in Bangladesh, largely driven by it youth, and characterized by a push for democracy and reforms. This movement, sometimes called the Students-People's Uprising or the July Revolution, emerged in the summer of 2024 following protests against a reinstated job quota system. The movement led to the resignation of Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina and her cabinet and the establishment of an interim government.
